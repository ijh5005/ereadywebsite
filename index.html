About Us

Entertainment Ready is a Entertainment company that currently specializes in single’s events that focus on safety and fun. We call this type of dating, “PG Dating”. We focus not on match making but on the security of our singles and encourage self discovery. We take dating experiences to an excitingly new level by reviving the word FUN, as a verb. We take your most memorable experiences and challenge them, we take your best you and make her shine.

By preserving the face-to-face dating experience and combining it with fun games designed to authenticate and rejuvenate, we have been able to create the best recipe for a lifetime of success stories.

Events

Entertainment Ready hosts singles’ events each month throughout the year. These events may include: Fun and familiar game shows such as; Family Feud and The Dating Game (customized for our singles).
